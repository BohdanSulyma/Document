Triple main camera

The main camera of the Samsung Galaxy A50 consists of three modules, each of which is responsible for its tasks. The 25 MP camera (F1.7) takes amazing photos in terms of clarity and brightness in any lighting. To obtain panoramic images, 8 MP camera resources with an ultra-wide lens and a viewing angle of 123 degrees are used. The third module at 5 MP is responsible for the depth of the frame. With the Galaxy A50, all your photos will be perfect.
